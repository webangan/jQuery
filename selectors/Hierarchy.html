Child Selector (“parent > child”)
$("div > .abc").css("background","#f00"); 		selects all direct child elements specified by "child" of elements specified by "parent".
Descendant Selector (“ancestor descendant”)
$("div .abc").css("background","#f00"); 		Selects all elements that are descendants of a given ancestor.
Next Adjacent Selector (“prev + next”)
$(".abc + div").css("background","#f00"); 		Selects all next elements matching "next" that are immediately preceded by a sibling "prev"
Next Siblings Selector (“prev ~ siblings”)
$(".abc ~ div").css("background","#f00"); 		Selects all sibling elements that follow after the "prev" element, have the same parent, and match the filtering "siblings" selector.
